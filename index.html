About Us

Kuriya Brothers Cotton Fibre Pvt. Ltd. is a private company incorporated in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, focused on manufacturing high-quality yarns. The company operates across two states, with its headquarters in Ahmedabad and a spinning unit in Vellakoil, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in cotton, polyester, and their byproducts.

Our Business Details

Founded on July 29, 2024, Kuriya Brothers Cotton Fibre Pvt. Ltd. is led by our partners:

  • Parshv Kuriya
  • Hitesh Kuriya
  • Krutika Kuriya
  • Preet Kuriya
The company has an authorized capital of ₹15 Lakh and operates in the cotton and polyester industries. With a focus on expanding its spinning unit, we aim to enhance capacity and efficiency.

Our products are distributed across multiple locations in Gujarat and Tamil Nadu, with branches in Vellakoil and Madurai.

Objectives

Our main objective is to strategically enhance and expand our spinning unit, incorporating advanced technologies and optimizing processes to meet growing demand and increase overall productivity.
