About Us

Incorporated on July 29, 2024 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Kuriya Brothers Cotton Fibre Pvt. Ltd. focuses on producing and trading high-quality yarns from cotton and polyester. The company operates across Gujarat and Tamil Nadu, with a spinning unit in Vellakoil and a warehouse in Naroda.

Our Business

  • Parent Company: Kuriya Brothers Textiles Pvt. Ltd.
  • Founded by: Parshv, Hitesh, Krutika, and Preet Kuriya
  • Authorised Capital: ₹15 Lakh
  • Trades in: Cotton, Polyester, and Byproducts
  • Branches: Ahmedabad (HQ), Vellakoil, Madurai
  • Goal: Scale and optimize our spinning unit for better productivity and efficiency

Spinning Unit Infrastructure

Our advanced spinning unit in Vellakoil, Tamil Nadu is being enhanced to meet growing demands with optimized processes, cutting-edge machinery, and skilled workforce training — increasing production capacity and quality.
